## Canon Review Areas

Every new output should be reviewed for:

- Mission alignment.
- Christian worldview.
- Masculine reconstruction.
- Spiritual vigilance.
- Watchman Universe consistency.
- Visual consistency.
- Writing style.
- Community doctrine.
- Future expansion compatibility.

---

## Archetype Rules

Archetypes are not superheroes.

They represent:

- Real masculine psychological states.
- Real spiritual struggles.
- Real dimensions of purpose.
- Real stages of refinement.

Do not add new archetypes without a canon review.

---

## Enemy Rules

The Forces of Corruption are personified destructive influences.

They are not shallow villains.
They are not traditional monsters.
They are not entertainment-first antagonists.

Official forces:

- The Whisperer
- The Scarlet Queen
- The Usurper
- Leviathan

---

## Visual Rules

Preserve:

- Dark cinematic realism.
- Grounded environments.
- Photographic realism.
- Atmospheric depth.
- Emotional restraint.
- The hooded Watchman.

Avoid:

- Cartoon aesthetics.
- Superhero aesthetics.
- Cyberpunk aesthetics.
- Neon-heavy palettes.
- Fantasy game armor.
- Occult or satanic symbols.
- Generic AI art.

---

## Writing Rules

Writing should feel:

- Direct.
- Masculine.
- Stoic.
- Serious.
- Spiritually grounded.
- Psychologically precise.
- Confrontational when necessary.
- Emotionally controlled.

Avoid:

- Corporate language.
- Self-help cliches.
- Soft motivation.
- Trend language.
- Influencer masculinity.

---

## Approval Checklist

Before publishing or building a new system:

1. Does it serve awakening rather than entertainment?
2. Does it preserve the Christian foundation?
3. Does it support discipline, truth, purpose, and vigilance?
4. Does it avoid visual drift?
5. Does it avoid writing drift?
6. Does it treat archetypes and enemies correctly?
7. Does it support the long-term ecosystem?

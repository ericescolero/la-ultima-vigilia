# ASSET REPLACEMENT GUIDE

## Purpose

This guide lists every placeholder image created for the redesigned La Ultima Vigilia website.

Replace these placeholders later with official Watchman Universe artwork.

Do not use stock photography, generic AI art, superhero imagery, cyberpunk imagery, occult symbols, or glossy fantasy-game character art.

Cinematic artwork placeholders and final cinematic artwork should use raster formats such as JPG or PNG. Use SVG only for logos, icons, seals, and interface symbols.

All final imagery should follow the visual reference direction:

- dark cinematic realism
- storm-lit atmosphere
- deep blacks
- charcoal and wet stone
- moonlit blue-gray
- faded gold
- sacred light emerging from darkness
- grounded Watchman Universe realism

## Replacement Rules

1. Keep filenames stable unless the HTML/CSS references are updated at the same time.
2. Preserve the recommended aspect ratios to avoid layout shifts.
3. Optimize final images for web delivery.
4. Provide separate desktop and mobile hero crops.
5. Use JPG or PNG for cinematic artwork; reserve SVG for logos, icons, seals, and interface symbols only.
6. Avoid bright fantasy colors, neon, cartoon effects, superhero energy, and occult symbols.
7. Keep the Watchman hooded, grounded, and realistic.
8. Use gold as sacred light or command, not decoration.
9. Keep emotional tone solemn, masculine, prophetic, and restrained.

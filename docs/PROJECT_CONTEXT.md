# PURPOSE

La Última Vigilia exists to awaken Christian men from spiritual sleep.
The project combines Christian discipleship, masculine development, spiritual warfare, discipline, storytelling, visual mythology, and modern media.
The objective is not entertainment.
The objective is awakening.
The project speaks to men who feel isolated, distracted, spiritually exhausted, addicted to comfort, trapped in cycles of temptation, or disconnected from their God-given purpose.

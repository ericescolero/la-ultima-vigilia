SUBCONSCIOUS WRITING PRINCIPLE
The strongest writing does not tell men what to do.
The strongest writing tells men who they have become.
Examples:
Weak:
"Stop wasting time."
Strong:
"You know exactly what you're capable of. That's why your current life frustrates you."
Weak:
"Be disciplined."
Strong:
"The problem isn't that you don't know what to do. The problem is that you've become comfortable knowing and not doing."
Weak:
"Work harder."
Strong:
"Some men are exhausted because they work hard. Others are exhausted because they spend years avoiding what needs to be done."
The subconscious responds to recognition before instruction.

EXPLICIT LANGUAGE RULES
Strategic explicit language is permitted when it increases emotional impact.
Profanity should never be used for shock.
It should be used as emphasis.
It should feel earned.
Examples:
"This is destroying you and you know it."
can become:
"This is destroying your damn life and you know it."
"You are distracted."
can become:
"You're wasting years of your life chasing stupid distractions."
The goal is not profanity.
The goal is force.

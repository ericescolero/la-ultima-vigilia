# EMAIL SEQUENCE ARCHITECTURE

## La Ultima Vigilia

Version: 1.0

---

## Purpose

The email system exists to move a man from first contact into deeper formation.

Primary objectives:

1. Deliver `La Guerra Silenciosa`.
2. Establish the mission of La Ultima Vigilia.
3. Diagnose the invisible war beneath distraction, comfort, desire, identity confusion, and pride.
4. Introduce El Remanente.
5. Prepare future paths into `La Guerra Interior`, courses, membership, and community.

The email system should not feel like a marketing newsletter. It should feel like a sequence of battlefield briefings.

---

## Canon Rules

All email copy must remain:

- Spanish-first
- Christian
- Direct
- Masculine
- Spiritually grounded
- Confrontational when necessary
- Emotionally controlled
- Mission-driven

Avoid:

- Corporate newsletter language
- Soft motivational language
- Self-help cliches
- Influencer masculinity
- Excessive positivity
- Trend language
- Entertainment-first framing

The emotional target is not hype. The target is recognition, conviction, clarity, and movement.

## Email Types

### Delivery Email

Must include:

- Confirmation of the requested resource.
- Download link.
- Short warning-style introduction.
- One next-step CTA.

Must avoid:

- Long brand story.
- Sales pitch.
- Multiple competing CTAs.

### Mission Email

Purpose:

- Explain why La Ultima Vigilia exists.
- Establish that the project is about awakening, discipline, spiritual warfare, and purpose.

### Enemy Diagnosis Emails

Each enemy email should include:

- Battlefield
- Weapon
- Signature lie
- Warning signs
- Countermeasure
- CTA to reflect, act, or continue reading

Enemy set:

- The Whisperer: mind, deception, distraction, compromise.
- The Scarlet Queen: desire, seduction, counterfeit intimacy.
- The Usurper: identity, inversion, forgotten purpose.
- Leviathan: pride, self-exaltation, rejection of correction.

### Community Invitation Email

Purpose:

- Introduce El Remanente as brotherhood and formation.
- Distinguish participants from passive consumers.
- Invite interest, waitlist, or application.

### Product Bridge Email

Purpose:

- Prepare for future `La Guerra Interior`, courses, or membership.
- Should be direct and restrained, not launch-hype driven.

# Blog System

Purpose:

- Plan, draft, review, and publish articles.
- Support the ten content pillars.
- Convert readers into the email funnel.

Canonical architecture:

```text
docs/BLOG_ARCHITECTURE.md
```

Folder roles:

- `drafts/`: unpublished article drafts.
- `published/`: approved article source copies.
- `categories/`: category definitions.
- `editorial-calendar/`: publishing schedule.
- `style-guides/`: article and voice guidance.

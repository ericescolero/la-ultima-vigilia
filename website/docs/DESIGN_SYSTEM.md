# DESIGN SYSTEM

## La Ultima Vigilia Website

---

## Visual Direction

The interface should feel like a digital watchtower:

- Dark.
- Cinematic.
- Restrained.
- Grounded.
- Serious.
- Spiritually intense.

---

## Color Tokens

Core palette:

- Deep black.
- Charcoal.
- Ash gray.
- Cold steel blue.
- Muted silver.
- Faded gold.
- Ember orange.

Avoid:

- Neon.
- Bright fantasy colors.
- Playful palettes.
- Corporate blue.

---

## Typography

Brand/headline direction:

- Cinzel preferred for final production.
- Serif fallback acceptable for static V1.
- Uppercase treatment should be used carefully and intentionally.

Body:

- Readable sans-serif.
- Strong line height.
- No tiny mobile text.

---

## Layout

Principles:

- Mobile-first.
- Strong negative space.
- Full-width atmospheric sections.
- Minimal nested cards.
- Email capture always easy to find.

---

## Components

Core components:

- Header.
- Footer.
- Hero.
- Email capture form.
- Lead magnet panel.
- Mission section.
- El Remanente section.
- Manual de Campo article card.
- Article layout.
- CTA section.

---

## Motion

Use little or no motion in Version 1.

If added later:

- Must respect reduced motion.
- Must feel cinematic and restrained.
- Must not feel trendy or flashy.

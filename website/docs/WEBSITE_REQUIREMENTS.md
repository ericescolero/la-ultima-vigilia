## Required User Outcomes

- Visitor understands the mission quickly.
- Visitor can submit an email.
- Visitor can access `La Guerra Silenciosa`.
- Visitor understands El Remanente as a serious brotherhood.
- Site can expand into Manuales de Campo, courses, membership, and archive systems.

---

## Required Forms

- Homepage hero form.
- Lead magnet page form.
- Manuales de Campo index form.
- Manual de Campo article form.
- El Remanente interest form.
- Final CTA form.

Every form must support:

- Empty state.
- Invalid email state.
- Loading state.
- Success state.
- Failure state.

## Required Quality

- Mobile-first.
- Fast loading.
- No horizontal overflow.
- Keyboard-accessible.
- Clear focus states.
- High contrast.
- SEO metadata.
- Canon-safe copy and visuals.
